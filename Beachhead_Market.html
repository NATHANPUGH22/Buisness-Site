Beachhead Market

Our target market

Features

Who has the most urgent need for my product/service?

Hobbyists and Makers who can't afford to buy CNC machines of their own or need a one off prototype for a project

What group of people would benefit the most from my product/service?

As I said before, the group of people this is geared to and I believe this would benefit the most are hobbyists and DIY enthusiasts as they can bring their creative ideas to life without investing in their own 3D printers.

Where do they spend their time (online or in person)?

My target audience might spend their time in places like Reddit on channels such as r/3Dprinting & r/DIY. They might also spend their time on YouTube (for tutorials), Facebook groups, and forums like Thingiverse or Printables for ideas.
